Team Snus


Team Snus is a professional esports and entertainment organization headquartered in Linköping, Sweden. Founded on May 23, 201, the organization has players from around Linköping, across multiple games, including Rocket League, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege, Overwatch, League Of Legends, Dead By Daylight and Fortnite Battle Royale.
